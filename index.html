Features

Everything your team needs

From workflow creation to team coordination — FlowSync has the tools to keep projects moving.

Workflow Management

Create, edit, and delete workflows with custom steps. Track status from Not Started → In Progress → Completed.

Task Assignment

Assign individual steps to team members, set due dates, and watch progress in real time across dashboards.
